Takeout Burger

A chopped burger seasoned like takeout

This burger is reminiscent of fast food burgers.

Very delicious with a heavy note of pickle but not overpowering

The chopping makes mixing all the ingredients easier but can be made into patties

Ingredients

  • 1 lb hamburger
  • 1 T oil
  • 1 T garlic powder
  • 1 T onion powder
  • 1/4 medium white onion
  • 1 1/2 T Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 pickle, chopped
  • 3 T pickle juice
  • 1 T dijon mustard
  • pepper to taste
  • ketchup (optional)
  • cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Set burner to med-high head, add oil and warm pan
  2. Add onions and saute for 3-5 min or until they start getting a little brown around the edges
  3. If the onions start to stick you can add the pickle juice or Worcestershire sauce early to deglaze
  4. Add burger and chopped up
  5. Leaving out the mustard and ketchup, add all the other ingredients
  6. With the hamburger mostly cooked(fine if it is fully cooked) add the mustard
  7. Cook 2 min or until a noticable decrease in steam occurs (you can take it off early, usually a bit runnier)
  8. Serve warm and can top with cheese and ketchup
